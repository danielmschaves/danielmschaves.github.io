Urban Futurability - Enel Group

Project Analyst

Urban Futurability is a project in Vila Olímpia, Brazil, which aims to create South America’s first Network Digital Twin. The 3D digital model will simulate the local electricity infrastructure, allowing for real-time monitoring of the grid's status. This technology will improve service quality by facilitating grid inspections, preventive maintenance, and identifying risks ahead of time. The project also aims to create an open, shared urban ecosystem, where energy becomes a service, promoting greater awareness in terms of energy use, efficiency, and savings.
